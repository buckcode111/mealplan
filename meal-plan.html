Weekly Shopping List

Protein

  • 18 large eggs
  • 500g lean ground beef
  • 500g chicken breast
  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 1 rotisserie chicken

Dairy

  • 2× 2L whole milk
  • 500g plain Greek yogurt
  • 200g cheddar block
  • 100g parmesan (grated)
  • Butter

Carbs

  • 500g rolled oats
  • 6× microwave rice pouches
  • 500g pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • Whole wheat bread (2 loaves)
  • 6× large whole wheat tortillas
  • Granola (400g bag)
  • Crackers / oatcakes
  • Instant mash (1 pack)

Fruit & Veg

  • 7 bananas
  • 4 oranges
  • 4 apples
  • Mixed berries (frozen bag, 400g)
  • Frozen broccoli (1kg bag)
  • Frozen corn (500g)
  • Frozen mixed veg (500g)
  • Baby spinach (300g bag)
  • Lettuce, cucumber

Pantry

  • Peanut butter (large jar)
  • Mixed nuts (200g)
  • Pesto (2 jars, green)
  • Sour cream (small tub)
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Honey
  • Garlic powder
  • Sesame oil (optional)
  • Orange juice (1L carton)
  • Mayo and/or mustard
💧 Drink roughly 2.5–3L of water daily on top of what you get from food and milk. With salmon removed, Vitamin D is now harder to hit from food — a daily supplement (1,000 IU) is strongly recommended. Scale up pasta and rice portions if your activity level is high.
